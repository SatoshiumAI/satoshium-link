# 🔗 Satoshium Gateway Layer

Satoshium Link serves as the navigation and routing layer of the Satoshium Universe.

It provides simple entry points into the broader ecosystem of platforms,
knowledge systems, experimental environments, infrastructure layers,
and coordination surfaces.

---

# 📡 Purpose

Complex ecosystems benefit from simple entry points.

The gateway layer exists to:

- connect domains across the Satoshium platform
- reduce navigation friction for visitors
- provide short routing paths to major systems
- support onboarding into the ecosystem
- act as a universal entry surface

It is not a content layer. It is a coordination surface.

---

# 🧭 Role Within the Platform

Satoshium is structured as a layered domain architecture:

satoshium.ai architecture layer
satoshium.info knowledge layer
satoshium.xyz experimental layer
satoshium.net infrastructure layer
satoshium.dev developer layer
satoshium.store economic layer
satoshium.link gateway layer

The gateway layer connects all other surfaces together.

---

# 🌐 Why This Domain Exists

As the Satoshium ecosystem expands across multiple domains and repositories,
navigation complexity increases.

The gateway layer helps:

- unify entry points
- simplify discovery
- provide stable routing paths
- support presentations and onboarding
- connect external audiences with platform layers

It functions as the connective surface of the Satoshium Universe.
