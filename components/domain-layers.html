🪐 Satoshium Universe: Domain Layers

Satoshium is expanding into a layered public ecosystem where each domain serves a distinct long-horizon role. Together, these domains form the public-facing architecture of the Satoshium Universe.

🌐 satoshium.ai

The main public platform layer of Satoshium. This is the primary brand surface where visitors encounter the broader vision, public systems, and AI-facing platform experience.

Visit satoshium.ai →

📚 satoshium.info

The knowledge and engineering workspace layer. This domain is intended to preserve structure, documentation, architecture, and public development surfaces that help explain how the ecosystem is built.

Visit satoshium.info →

🧪 satoshium.xyz

The experimental frontier of the Satoshium Universe. This is where new prototypes, agents, simulations, and exploratory concepts can be tested before they mature into more permanent layers.

Visit satoshium.xyz →

🛰️ satoshium.net

The future infrastructure and network layer. This domain is intended to hold service topology, node concepts, technical visibility, and the broader infrastructure perspective of the ecosystem.

Visit satoshium.net →

🛍️ satoshium.store

The merchandise and economic layer. This domain represents the future cultural and physical expression of Satoshium through symbolic products, artifacts, and community-facing brand presence.

Visit satoshium.store →

🔗 satoshium.link

The gateway and routing layer. This domain can eventually serve as a simple public entry point into the Satoshium Universe through redirects, navigation hubs, link routing, and QR-friendly access paths.

Visit satoshium.link →

🛠️ satoshium.dev

The future developer layer. This domain is intended for builder onboarding, SDKs, APIs, agent tooling, examples, and technical resources for those constructing systems within the Satoshium ecosystem.

Visit satoshium.dev/ →

🇺🇸 satoshium.us

The future U.S. operational layer. This domain could eventually support jurisdiction-specific topics such as regulatory context, compliance, public policy interfaces, and U.S.-aligned infrastructure explanations.

Visit satoshium.us/ →

In the long run, these domains may function together as distinct public layers of one coherent Satoshium Universe: platform, knowledge, experimentation, infrastructure, commerce, routing, development, and jurisdictional presence.
