Why This Domain Exists

Complex ecosystems benefit from simple entry points. Satoshium Link exists to make the growing Satoshium Universe easier to navigate, especially as new domains, repositories, and systems continue to emerge.

This domain is designed to reduce friction between visitors and the systems they want to explore.

The Role in the Ecosystem

Within the broader domain architecture, Satoshium Link acts as a lightweight navigation and routing layer connecting the major public surfaces of the project.

Think of this site as the gateway that connects all Satoshium domains.

Satoshium Domain Architecture

  • satoshium.ai — public platform layer
  • satoshium.info — knowledge and documentation layer
  • satoshium.xyz — experimental systems
  • satoshium.net — infrastructure and network layer
  • satoshium.store — economic and merchandise layer
  • satoshium.link — gateway and routing layer
Together these domains form the expanding architecture of the Satoshium Universe.
